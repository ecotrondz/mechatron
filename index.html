What Is Mechatron waste Management Machine & How It Works?


It's an innovative way to collect empty beverage containers at the point of use and boost recycling rates through a system of incentives. When recyclable materials like plastic, glass bottles, or aluminum beverage cans are thrown into the smart machine at the point where the waste occurs, it becomes possible for users to preserve and collect the waste individually and cleanly. The pre-established rewarding ways can be chosen by users to receive cash. Mechatron is ready to be implemented globally in both deposit and non-deposit scenarios.

TECHNICAL FEATURES

Telecommunication

Communicates information collected in real time through wireless transmission to the intelligent Waste management platform of MECHATRON, The 3G and 4G telecommunications modules are available through WCDMA and GSM networks.

Compaction

It has an integrated and patented solar-powered compactor, which is automatically activated based on the filling level of the bin. It is compatible with standard containers 120L
and 240L / MGB for easy and safe removal of waste.

Data and analysis in real time

The information about compaction and filling level is sent to CCN, which not only helps operational managers understand the location and hours of waste collections, but also
improves the way they collect waste by recommending optimal routes of collection
based on real-time data and predictive analytics.
